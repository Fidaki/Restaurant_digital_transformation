Roccos Dessert vegan and allergy friendly menu

Hospitality and customer experience

At Roccos Dessert I helped develop a new vegan and allergy-friendly dessert menu. The goal was to create desserts that were nut-free, dairy-free and suitable for people with allergies while still tasting like classic Roccos desserts.

I researched allergy trends, vegan options and customer feedback. I tested dairy-free alternatives like oat cream, coconut milk and vegan chocolate and shared findings with the kitchen team.

The outcome was a separate menu section that made ordering easier and safer for customers with specific dietary needs.

West Ivy problem solving page

Digital transformation for a restaurant

My final project is the creation of the West Ivy Problem-Solving page. I designed the layout, interactive features and digital elements to represent a real restaurant transformation scenario.

The design uses a beige colour system to match the rest of my website and keep the style consistent. The page includes galleries, opening status indicators, a booking form and contact features.

I added live interaction such as filters, toggles and a feedback form to show realistic customer features without needing a database.
